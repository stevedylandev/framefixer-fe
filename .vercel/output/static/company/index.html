My name is Steve, a self-taught developer from Chattanooga with an itch to solve problems through programming. I also have a deep history in photography, and I've learned a lot from years of shooting professionally and working with other photographers.


Something I observed lately as my wife edited wedding galleries with multiple shooters, is that almost every time she had to fix the timestamp on thousands of photos. It was long and tricky work in Lightroom and she always complained about it.


Well that's how I got here building FrameFixer, with the goal of helping other photographers save time and money when it comes to fixing this small problem.
